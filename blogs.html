Petra, Jordan

Series:

Wonders of the World


Description:

The ruins of a historic city, carved into rock, nestled within mountains and canyons, that was once home to over 30,000 people. There is a lot more to explore than the famous treasury, with 5 miles of structures to explore.

Chichen Itza, Mexico

Series:

Wonders of the World


Description:

An ancient Mayan city whose unique design and architecture provide an insight into the civilisation by which it was built. It is most known for the impressive Kulkulkan Pyramid at the centre of the site.

Machu Picchu, Peru

Series:

Wonders of the World


Description:

An Inca citadel situated high-up in the Andes Mountains of Peru. The site highlights the incredible construction and engineering skills of the Incan people, evidenced by its impressive preservation.

Taj Mahal, India

Series:

Wonders of the World


Description:

This eye-catching marble mausoleum was built in the memory of the wife of a Mughal Emperor. One of the best and most famous examples of Mughal architecture and a must-visit when in Agra.
